Hello, your Galaxy Docker GraphClust container is running!

Galaxy-GraphClust is a web-base workflow for structural clustering of RNA secondary structures developed on GraphClust methodology using the Galaxy framework. This is a running instance Galaxy-GraphClust virtualized container.

How to start:

A quick and easy way to get familiar with Galaxy and Galaxy-GraphClust would be to visit the interactive tours provided under Help → Interactive Tours. Or simply click on the Guided Tour button below to start a tour.

About Galaxy project:
