Hello, your Galaxy Docker GraphClust container is running!

Galaxy-GraphClust is a workflow for structural clustering of RNA secondary structures developed as an extension of GraphClust clustering pipeline [Heyne et al. 10.1093/bioinformatics/bts224] inside the Galaxy framework. It contains a set of integrated Galaxy tools and different flavors of clustering workflows built upon these tools.
